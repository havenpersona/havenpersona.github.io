Telematic Concert as a Technical Team Member.

Haven Kim
24 Jan 2021

On January 23, I participated in a telematic concert (remote concert) by College of Music at the Seoul National University “Unlimited Sense - Warmth of Non Contact “as a technical team member. Technical team members tried their best to minimize latency (a time delay) and give technical support for musicians from California and South Korea. The concert was recorded and it is available on YouTube.

I appreciate Professor Mark Dresser for giving me this opportunity.
