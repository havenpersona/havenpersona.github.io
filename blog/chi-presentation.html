Presented Our Work at CHI 2022

Haven Kim
03 May 2022

Due to COVID-19, I participated in CHI 2022 remotely. Due to the time difference, I had to wake up very early in the morning (like 5:00 AM). I presented our work, the melody of the mysterious stones, as a main presenter. I appreciate all those meaningful questions and comments for our work. I would also like to show my appreciation to Jaeran for her work and Professor Young Yim Doh for taking a screenshot of our presentation.
