The Melody of the Mysterious Stones

Virtual Reality

The Melody of Mysterious Stones is a VR meditation game where the players should use spatialized sounds to guide their way. I participated in this project as a software devleoper, sound director, co-director, and co-story developer. This project was presented in CHI 2022. Check out our paper for the details.
