Picturesque Instruments

Korean Traditional Virtual Instruments

I developed the Daegeum, Sogeum, and Danso virtual instruments. The Daegeum, the Sogeum, and the Danso are Korean traditional wood instruments. This project includes recording the instruments as well as  software development. I received ICAM Research Award 2021 for this project.
